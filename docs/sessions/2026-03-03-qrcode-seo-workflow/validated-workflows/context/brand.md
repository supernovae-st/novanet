# QRCode-AI Brand Guidelines

## Brand Voice
- **Tone**: Professional yet accessible
- **Style**: Technical accuracy with clear explanations
- **Language**: Avoid jargon, explain technical terms

## Key Messages
1. **Simplicity**: "Create QR codes in seconds"
2. **Power**: "Enterprise-grade features for everyone"
3. **Trust**: "Millions of QR codes generated"

## Target Audience
- Small business owners
- Marketing professionals
- Event organizers
- Developers integrating QR codes

## SEO Focus
- Action-oriented keywords ("create", "generate", "make")
- Feature keywords ("custom", "dynamic", "tracking")
- Use case keywords ("menu", "wifi", "vcard")
