IMPLEMENTATION:      2-3 days effort

   Domain authority                    7.5% correlation  ← Focus here
   Backlinks                           6.2% correlation  ← Then here
   Content quality                     3.1% correlation  ← Then here

3. NOVANET'S ADR-032 ARCHITECTURE IS OPTIMAL
   ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

   Your pattern:

   /qr-code-generator              (parent, invariant)
   /qr-code-generator/instagram    (child, locale-specific)

   Competitive analysis:

   ~30% of competitors:  Flat, repetitive URLs (worse)
                        /qr-generator-para-instagram ❌

   ~50% of competitors:  Parent/child structure (like you)
                        /qr-generator/instagram ✅

   ~20% of competitors:  Confusing deep nesting (worse)
                        /tools/generators/qr/instagram ❌

   Conclusion: You're at/ahead of market. Keep ADR-032 as-is.

FAQ
================================================================================

Q: Will changing "codigo" to "código" hurt rankings?
A: No. Google treats them as canonically equivalent. 301 redirects
   handle the transition. You may see initial fluctuation (normal).

Q: What if competitors aren't using accents?
A: You'll have competitive advantage. Be first to use Google's 2024
   recommendation. Early adopters see CTR gains before competitors.

Q: Why is URL slug impact so small?
A: Because content quality is 100× more important. URLs are hygiene
   factor, not primary ranking lever. Focus most effort on content.

Q: Should we do this now or wait?
A: Implement soon. Google is already rewarding UTF-8 accents. First-mover
   advantage = +1-2% CTR before competitors catch on.

Q: What's the implementation timeline?
A: 2-3 days for development + testing. 1 day staging. 1 day production
   deploy + monitoring setup. Total: 4-6 days if doing full cycle.

Q: What if it goes wrong?
A: Rollback plan included. 301 redirects from new → old URLs. Database
   backup before migration. Test in staging first.
